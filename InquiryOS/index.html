How to use InquiryOS

Getting Started

To begin an inquiry, choose "Start Inquiry" and enter your name. If your teacher provided a class code, enter it to join their shared session.

[Onboarding Screen Screenshot]

Creating Models

Use the Models module to drag nodes onto the canvas. Use the draw tool to connect them and explain how different parts of a system interact.

[Modeling Canvas Screenshot]

Analyzing Data

Input your observations into the data tables. InquiryOS will automatically generate charts to help you identify patterns and trends in your investigation.

[Data Table & Chart Screenshot]

Claim-Evidence-Reasoning

Synthesize your findings by making a claim, selecting evidence from your data, and explaining your reasoning based on scientific principles.

[CER Framework Screenshot]
